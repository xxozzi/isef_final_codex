\section*{CDA Expected Ablation Tables}

All values in this document are \emph{expected placeholders}, not measured results.
They are aligned to the expected CDA headline numbers used in
\texttt{cda\_swad\_style\_tables.tex} and to the qualitative ordering defended in the
paper: the canalization term is expected to matter most, the mergeability term and
worst-domain source fit should matter materially, and exact optimized simplex weights
should matter less than finding the right support.
